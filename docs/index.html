Overview

This Wiki grew out of my study for the Oracle Certified Master (OCM) 10G Exam. I had originally recorded my knowledge in a blog, but the search facility for that blogging platform was too slow to be useful. The Wiki format proved to be more suitable for my needs.

Since then, the Wiki has been exepanded to cover the 11G and 12C OCM Upgrade Exams as well as other Oracle RDBMS certifications. Once the OCM 23C Exam is released, I will record my study notes in this Wiki.

Description of Contents

Exam Topics

The heart of this Wiki is arranged around the various certification exams. Each exam has its own documentation sub-tree with the anchor page is a summary of exam topics with my self-assessment on a scale of one (1) to five (5). One (1) is assigned to topics that I have no knowledge, and five (5) is assigned to topics that I am very comfortable with.

Build Journals

I recorded the progress of builds for various as journals within this Wiki. Exam-specific builds are recorded under the various exam sub-trees. Build journals for common systems under recorded under System Builds.
