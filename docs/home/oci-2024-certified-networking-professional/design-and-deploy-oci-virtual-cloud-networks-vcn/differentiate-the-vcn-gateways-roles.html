There are at least three (3) VCN Gateway roles:

  1. DRG: Dynamic Routing Gateway is used to route between the private on-premise network, or to a VCN in another region.
  2. Internet: Internet Gateway is used to route between a VCN and the Internet.
  3. LPG: Local Peering Gateway is used to route between VCNs in the same region.
