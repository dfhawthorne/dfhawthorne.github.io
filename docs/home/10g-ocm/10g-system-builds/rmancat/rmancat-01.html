Thursday 05 June, 2008 - 23:01

Cloned RHEL 4.2 1Gb Base as RMANCAT . Attached the ethernet NIC to the VMNET0 bridge. Added five (5) disks:
(1) /dev/sdb (8 Gb) for /u00 filesystem
(2) /dev/sdc and /dev/sdd (4 Gb each) for the DATA ASM disk group
(3) /dev/sde and /dev/sdf (8 Gb each) for the FRA ASM disk group

Tried installing the OMS agent but got an error during the agent configuration.

Changed the initdefault to 3 and shut the image down. Will look at the errors in the morning.
